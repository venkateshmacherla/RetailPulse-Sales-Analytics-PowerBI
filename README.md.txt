# RetailPulse Sales Analytics Dashboard

RetailPulse Sales Analytics is an interactive Power BI dashboard project built to explore retail sales data and turn raw transactional information into meaningful business insights.

The dashboard covers sales trends, product performance, customer behaviour, regional performance, inventory movement, orders, and other key business metrics.

I built this project with a focus on creating a clean and easy-to-understand reporting experience where users can explore the data using interactive filters and multiple analytical views.

## Dashboard Overview

The report contains multiple dashboard pages, with each page focusing on a different area of the retail business.

### 1. Executive Summary
Provides a high-level overview of important business KPIs and overall sales performance.

### 2. Sales Trend Analysis
Analyzes how sales and orders change over time, including monthly and yearly trends.

### 3. Product Performance
Helps identify top-performing products and understand product-level sales performance.

### 4. Customer Analytics
Provides insights into customer activity, orders, sales contribution, and customer-level performance.

### 5. Country / Regional Sales
Compares sales, orders, and customers across different countries and regions.

### 6. Monthly & Seasonal Sales
Analyzes monthly patterns and seasonal changes in sales, orders, and quantity sold.

### 7. Customer Behaviour
Explores customer purchasing behaviour using order frequency, sales per customer, and country-level customer analysis.

### 8. Profit & Revenue Analysis
Provides an analytical view of sales and estimated profit-related metrics across countries and time periods.

### 9. Inventory Risk
Identifies fast-moving and slow-moving products and analyzes product movement based on quantity sold and sales contribution.

### 10. Orders & Transactions
Analyzes order trends, order value, quantity, countries, and high-value transactions.

### 11. Advanced Analytics
Uses analytical visuals such as scatter plots, treemaps, and contribution analysis to explore relationships within the retail data.

### 12. Interactive Filter
Provides a flexible report view where users can explore the data using filters such as year, month, country, quarter, and product.

## Key KPIs

Some of the main KPIs used across the dashboard include:

- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Total Products
- Total Quantity Sold
- Average Order Value
- Sales per Customer
- Sales per Order
- Profit Margin %
- Highest Product Revenue
- Highest Quantity Sold
